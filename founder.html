Escolhares - Bringing Vision to Children

Escolhares is the Swiss-Brazilian nonprofit I co-founded to deliver comprehensive eye care to schoolchildren in Rio de Janeiro. Our organisation manages the full pathway from screening to eyeglass delivery, reaching more than 4,000 children annually and over 20,000 children since inception.

How the Program Works

  • School-based visual screening and early identification
  • Clinical referral pathway for ophthalmology review
  • Prescription and eyeglass delivery with follow-up support
  • Digital tracking to monitor outcomes and continuity of care

Why Escolhares Matters

  • Improves learning outcomes through better visual access
  • Reduces barriers to care in underserved communities
  • Connects healthcare, schools, and families in one model
  • Builds a scalable public-health framework for pediatric vision
